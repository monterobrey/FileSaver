Mt. Pulag Hiking Trail

Package Inclusions

  • Round-trip transportation from Baguio City to Mt. Pulag Ranger Station
  • Local guide and Mt. Pulag registration fees
  • Camping equipment (tents, sleeping bags, and mats)
  • Environmental fee and hiking permits
  • Basic orientation and safety briefing

Schedule:

Day 1

  • 10:00 PM - Meet-up in Baguio City (specified pick-up point)
  • 11:00 PM - Depart for Mt. Pulag Ranger Station

Day 2

  • 3:00 AM - Arrive at Ranger Station and orientation
  • 3:30 AM - Begin ascent to the summit (approximately 3-4 hours)
  • 6:30 AM - Arrive at Mt. Pulag summit; enjoy sunrise and scenic views
  • 7:30 AM - Descend to Ranger Station
  • 10:30 AM - Breakfast at Ranger Station
  • 11:30 AM - Depart for Baguio City
  • 3:00 PM - Arrive back in Baguio City

Note:

  • Minimum of 4 participants required for the tour
  • Bring warm clothing (temperatures at the summit can drop below 10°C)
  • Hiking difficulty: Moderate (not suitable for first-time hikers or children under 12)
  • Tour subject to weather conditions and DENR guidelines
